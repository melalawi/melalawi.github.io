Unofficial Ultimate-Guitar Enhancer

Summary

Ultimate-Guitar.com is a very large website geared towards musicians such as myself (although most aren't as mediocre as I am). I've been a part of this community for over five years now, and despite how archaic and deprecated the forum's layout was (images often skewed the layout of threada, users were restricted to tiny, insignificant avatars, a number of features were simply broken, and customization was next to none), I stuck around. Eventually, I decided to respond to the countless unanswered feature requests and bug reports myself by creating a browser extension. I started off small, with only the goal of giving users the ability to upload larger avatar images to their accounts, but eventually the scope of this project grew at an exciting (but still manageable!) rate. At this point, UUGE is capable of:

  • Larger Forum Avatars
  • Embedding Modern Media Types, such as .webm
  • Displaying Images within Post Signatures
  • Hiding Posts and Threads Made by Ignored Users
  • Custom Forum Colours/CSS
